About Me

I am currently a third year Bachelor of Mathematics student at the Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore. My high schooling was from Delhi Public School, Ruby Park Kolkata. My research interests include Differential Geometry and Functional Analysis, as well as related disciplines like Probability and Programming. Apart from ISI Bangalore, I have done Summer Projects in TIFR-CAM and will be interning in IISc Bangalore this winter. My hobbies include playing my violin, reading books (both factual and fiction), superhero comics and football.

Research Projects

ISI Bangalore KVPY Project

Prof. Yogeshwaran Dhandapani, ISI Bangalore • May 2015 - July 2015

I did a 2 month project under Prof. Yogeshwaran Dhandapani on a non-measure theoretic proof of the Strong Law of Large Numbers and Fekete's Lemma and it's corollaries. I also read the book "Generatingfunctionology" by Herbert Wilf and solved exercises as instructed by my guide.

VSRP at TIFR-CAM

Prof. K Sandeep, TIFR-CAM • May 2016 - July 2016

I am working under Prof. K Sandeep for 2 months. It was primarily an advanced comprehensive reading project on the topics of Differential Geometry, Calculus on Manifolds, Theory of Differential Equations and the beginnings of Functional Analysis. I have not been acquainted with any of these subjects formally before, and hence thoroughly enjoyed this project. I am also solving problems as directed by my guide.

Winter Internship at IISc Bangalore
